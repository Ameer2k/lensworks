How Ordering Works

  1. Open a delivered gallery and mark favorites or choose individual images.
  2. Select product type, finish, framing, and shipping destination.
  3. Review crops carefully for vertical and panoramic images before checkout.
  4. Track production and delivery from your dashboard once the order is placed.

Production & Shipping

Most print orders are produced within 3 to 5 business days. Albums and framed products may take longer due to assembly and finish checks. Shipping speed depends on the destination selected during checkout, and tracking is shared once the order is dispatched.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order directly from any gallery?

Only delivered galleries with print ordering enabled by the vendor can be used for print checkout.

Do vendors approve crops?

Core image edits are vendor-approved already, but crop previews remain visible at checkout so you can review them.

Can I ship to another address?

Yes. Gift deliveries and alternate shipping destinations can be selected during final checkout.

What if a print arrives damaged?

Contact support with your order details and clear photos of the packaging and item so a replacement review can begin.

Display Planning Guide

Single Hero Piece

Best when one image carries the full story: engagement portraits, iconic wedding frames, or brand hero photography.

Grid Wall Set

Use multiple coordinated prints when you want a richer narrative sequence rather than a single focal frame.

Album Storytelling

Choose albums for milestone sessions where chronology, pacing, and tactile presentation matter as much as the images.

Ordering advice

Start with the images you already marked as favorites.

Favorites are the fastest way to build a print shortlist because the strongest hero images usually reveal themselves during gallery review. From there, choose whether you want one statement piece, a sequence, or a giftable set.

Start small

Test one framed print before committing to a larger wall series.

Plan for space

Think about room size, lighting, and frame finish before checkout.

Protect keepsakes

Albums and archival papers are best for milestone moments you want to preserve long-term.
