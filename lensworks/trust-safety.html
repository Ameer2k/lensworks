Trust & Safety

LensWorks is built to keep bookings, payments, and communication on-platform wherever possible. These standards help protect both clients and vendors before, during, and after every session.

Verified Profiles

Vendor profiles should contain accurate identity, portfolio, service, and payout information before bookings are accepted.

Protected Payments

Deposits and payouts should move through approved payment processors so booking status and receipts stay tied to the transaction record.

Recorded Communication

Use platform messaging when discussing scope, timing, or disputes so there is a clear record if support review is needed.

For Clients

  • Review vendor ratings, galleries, turnaround times, and package details before paying a deposit.
  • Keep event details, venue requirements, and guest restrictions accurate in the booking record.
  • Never send payment outside the platform unless the transaction is explicitly managed off-platform by contract.
  • Report threatening, deceptive, or discriminatory conduct immediately through support.

For Vendors

  • Maintain truthful portfolios and clear package descriptions that match the services you actually deliver.
  • Respond promptly to booking requests and avoid moving critical conversations to untracked channels.
  • Respect privacy, venue rules, and the safety of all participants during session coverage.
  • Escalate fraud concerns, harassment, or suspicious payment activity before the shoot date whenever possible.

Report A Concern

Abuse, Fraud, or Safety Risk

If you believe a listing is fraudulent, a payment request is suspicious, or a message creates a safety concern, contact support with the booking ID, involved account, and a clear description of what happened. Urgent risks should also be reported to local authorities where appropriate.
