Privacy Policy

LensWorks collects only the information needed to create accounts, manage bookings, process payments, deliver galleries, and keep communication between clients and vendors secure. By using the platform, you agree to the handling described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect account details such as your name, email address, phone number, profile photo, vendor business information, booking history, messages sent through the platform, and gallery access activity. Payment details are processed through trusted payment providers and are not stored in full on LensWorks servers.

2. How We Use Data

Your information is used to create and manage accounts, confirm bookings, send receipts, facilitate client-vendor communication, deliver galleries, improve search relevance, detect suspicious behavior, and provide customer support.

3. Payment and Financial Data

Payments, deposits, and payouts may be handled by third-party processors such as Stripe. These providers maintain their own security and compliance standards. LensWorks receives only the metadata needed to confirm payment status, reconcile receipts, and route vendor payouts.

4. Messages, Galleries, and Shared Content

Messages sent through LensWorks may be retained to support booking records, dispute review, and fraud prevention. Gallery assets, favorites, and download actions may also be logged so clients and vendors can review delivery status and access history.

5. Marketing Preferences

If you opt into newsletters or platform updates, LensWorks may send marketing emails about product improvements, seasonal campaigns, or featured vendors. You can opt out of promotional email at any time from account settings or from the unsubscribe link in those messages.

6. Your Choices

You may update profile information, adjust notification preferences, request account changes, and contact support for data deletion or export requests where permitted by law. Some records may be retained for fraud prevention, tax, legal, or contractual obligations.
