Frequently Asked Questions

When is my booking confirmed?

A booking is confirmed once the vendor accepts the request and the deposit is successfully paid. You will then see the booking move into your dashboard with the confirmed date, time, and receipt number.

Can I reschedule after paying a deposit?

Yes, reschedules are allowed when the vendor is available and the request is made within the reschedule window described in the cancellation policy. Some packages may carry a date-change fee for last-minute moves.

How do gallery downloads work?

Delivered galleries include web-resolution previews and download access based on the package purchased. Some vendors also enable ZIP delivery, print ordering, and favorites collections for shared albums.

What should vendors do if a client stops responding?

Keep all communication inside LensWorks, document attempted follow-ups, and contact seller support if the booking date is approaching. This keeps the record auditable in case a dispute needs review.
