Before Opening a Case

  • Keep messages and booking updates on-platform where possible.
  • Include the booking ID, client name, and a short timeline of what happened.
  • Attach payout dates, screenshots, or delivery notes if the issue involves money or fulfillment.
  • State what outcome you are requesting: payout review, cancellation review, or listing fix.

Recommended Escalation Sequence

1. Keep the record on-platform

Use LensWorks messaging for key updates so support can audit the conversation if the case escalates.

2. Gather proof once

Compile one clear timeline with screenshots, booking IDs, payout references, or delivery notes rather than sending fragmented follow-ups.

3. Request a concrete outcome

Support resolves faster when the request is specific: release payout, review dispute, restore listing visibility, or confirm verification status.
