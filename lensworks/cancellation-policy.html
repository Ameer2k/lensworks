Cancellation Policy

This policy explains how deposits, date changes, refunds, and vendor no-show situations are handled for bookings made through LensWorks. Individual vendor package terms may add stricter conditions only where clearly disclosed before checkout.

14+ Days Before

Client may request cancellation with maximum refund eligibility, less non-refundable service fees where disclosed.

3 to 13 Days Before

Partial refund or credit may apply depending on package, prep work, and vendor availability impact.

Within 72 Hours

Deposits are usually non-refundable unless the vendor cannot perform or support grants an exception.

Rescheduling

Date changes are encouraged over cancellation when possible. If the vendor is available for the new date, LensWorks may allow the deposit to transfer to the new booking record. Last-minute date changes may carry a rescheduling fee or revised package terms.

Vendor No-Show or Inability to Perform

If a vendor cancels, fails to appear, or cannot reasonably perform the confirmed service, the client may be eligible for a full deposit refund and platform support review. Where possible, LensWorks may also help identify replacement availability.

Non-Refundable Fees

Some platform or payment-processing fees may remain non-refundable even when a booking is canceled. These costs should be displayed clearly during checkout or referenced in the final receipt.
