1. Marketplace Role

LensWorks helps clients discover vendors, compare packages, request bookings, pay deposits, and manage post-booking communication. Unless expressly stated, LensWorks is not the direct provider of photography services and does not guarantee vendor output beyond the policies and protections published on the platform.

2. Booking and Payment Terms

Bookings are subject to vendor acceptance, availability, and successful payment authorization. Deposit amounts, remaining balances, service fees, and any add-ons are shown before checkout. Clients are responsible for reviewing session details before payment is submitted.

3. Vendor Responsibilities

Vendors must keep listing details accurate, communicate professionally, honor confirmed bookings except where cancellation rules apply, and deliver the services promised in their package descriptions. Fraudulent listings, fake portfolios, abusive behavior, or repeated delivery failures may lead to removal.

4. Client Responsibilities

Clients must provide accurate booking information, timely event details, lawful and safe venues, and respectful communication. Clients are also responsible for reviewing vendor package scope, turnaround times, and cancellation terms before booking.

5. Disputes and Moderation

Disputes should first be addressed through the LensWorks messaging channel so a clear record exists. LensWorks may review communications, booking metadata, and payment status to make platform decisions, but vendor-client contractual obligations may still require direct resolution where local law applies.

6. Changes to the Platform

LensWorks may update features, fees, policies, or vendor tools as the service evolves. Material changes to core terms or policy documents will be reflected on the site and may also be sent through email or in-product notices for registered users.
